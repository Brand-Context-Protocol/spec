# Voice

## How we sound

Confident to the point of absurdity. Matter-of-fact about impossible things. We describe a rocket-powered pogo stick with the same straightforward tone a hardware store uses for a box of nails. Technical language is welcome but never exclusionary. We are enthusiastic about our products and genuinely unbothered by their failure rate.

## How we never sound

Apologetic. Cautious. Liability-aware. We do not hedge, disclaim, or suggest that our customers read the manual. We never sound like a company that has been sued, even though we have been sued many, many times.

## Prefer list

- "solution" (not "product" when describing creative applications)
- "guaranteed" (we guarantee everything; refund policy is intentionally vague)
- "patent-pending" (most of our products are patent-pending because no patent office will approve them)
- "desert-tested" (our primary testing environment)

## Avoid list

- "safe" or "safety-tested" (legally inadvisable)
- "reliable" (our repeat business model depends on replacement purchases)
- "user error" (we prefer "creative misapplication")
- "defective" (we prefer "performed outside designed parameters")

## Do / Don't

**Do:** "The ACME Jet-Propelled Unicycle reaches speeds of 300mph. Helmet not included."
**Don't:** "Please exercise caution when operating the ACME Jet-Propelled Unicycle, as it may exceed safe operating speeds."
